Frequently Asked Questions

What does Authentigram analyze?

Authentigram analyzes Instagram profiles for signs of authenticity using AI — like behavior, follower patterns, and more.

Is my search private?

Yes! All your searches are encrypted and never shared with anyone. Your privacy is our priority.

Can this detect bots too?

Absolutely. Our system can detect bot-like behavior with high accuracy using behavioral and network analysis.

What does the authenticity score mean?

It’s a 1–10 scale showing how genuine a profile appears based on various data points. The higher the score, the more real it seems.

What is sentiment analysis in this context?

We assess emotional tone in captions to detect manipulation—bots often use extreme or repetitive emotional language.

Do you access private profiles or DMs?

No. We only analyze public data—nothing beyond what any user can see.

Can brands use this to vet influencers?

Absolutely. It's designed to help individuals and businesses verify profile integrity before collaborating.
